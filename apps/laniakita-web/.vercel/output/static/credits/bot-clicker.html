Big Thank you to all these rad artists who made the following works open-source/royalty free!

Models

"BOT NEIL", by Oscar Creativo

License: CC 4.0

All of the adorable little red robots were created by this amazing artist and you should definitely check out more of their work at the link above!

The ".glb" file that stores the model was compressed with gltfjsx, so you can witness them on a web browser.

Beyond that, there's not actually any hard changes to them. Everything else is done entirely with Three.js sending the math to your device to compute their positional & rotational vectors in time and space, as well as how light and shadow interacts with them.

Sounds

FilmCow Royalty Free Sound Effects Library Vol. 4 by FilmCow

Massive thank you to FilmCow! When you click on a Bot, there's a chance you'll hear some of Jason Steele's incredible voice work, which you can find more of at FilmCow.com.

Pixel UI Sound Effects by Atelier Magicae

Big thank you to Atelier Magicae! When you click on a Bot there's a chance to hear these really beautiful sounds created by them. You can find even more of their amazing work at Atelier Magicae's shopfront on itch.io.

Libraries

Three.js, by Mr. Doob

Three.js is a really elegant and convenient library for WebGL. Thank you to @mrdoob!

react-three/(fiber, drei, a11y), gltfjsx, zustand, by Poimandres

Thank you to the pmndrs collective for all of their hardwork creating these amazing component libraries for Three.js + the react ecosystem!
