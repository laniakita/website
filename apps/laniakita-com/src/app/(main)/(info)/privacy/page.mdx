1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Lani Akita ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website, laniakita.com (the "Website"). It also explains the choices you have about your information and how you can contact us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you:

Information you provide directly: This includes information you provide when you comment on a post, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us. This information may include your name, email address, and any other information you choose to share.
Automatically collected information: When you use the Website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time spent on the Website.
Cookies: We use cookies to store information about your preferences and to track your activity on the Website. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device. You can opt out of non-essential cookies by adjusting your browser settings.
Authentication Cookie: We use a session cookie to validate your login if you create an account. This cookie is essential for maintaining your logged-in status and expires when you close your browser.
Local Storage: We use local storage to store your preferred theme selection for the Website. Local storage allows us to remember your preference for future visits and improve your user experience.
IndexedDB: We use IndexedDB to cache website content offline. This allows you to access certain content even when you are not connected to the internet. No personal information is stored in IndexedDB. 3. Use of Your Information
